Questions

Common questions.

Who is Velago built for?
Independent financial advisors who want to communicate more consistently with clients and prospects — without spending hours writing every report, post, and newsletter themselves.
Is the content compliant with financial regulations?
All outputs are generated as drafts for your review. You are responsible for reviewing content for compliance with applicable regulations in your jurisdiction before sending to clients. We produce the content — you approve it.
How do I submit my client data?
Through a simple, secure intake form. No software to install. No integrations required. Fill out the form with your client data and performance figures — we handle everything from there.

You do. All content generated for your account is fully yours — no co-branding, no attribution, no restrictions on use.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account — no notice period required. Your access continues until the end of your paid billing period.
What if I need changes to an output?
Request revisions by replying to your delivery email. We update and return outputs within 24 hours of your revision request.
