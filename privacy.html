Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  NEA  ·  velago.app

This Privacy Policy describes how NEA ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the VelaGo platform ("Service") at velago.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and payment details provided during registration.
  • Submitted data: Client information and financial data you submit through our intake forms for the purpose of generating outputs.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns within the platform.
  • Communication data: Emails and messages you send to our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Deliver the AI-generated outputs you requested
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our outputs
  • Send service-related notifications (not marketing, unless opted in)

3. Client Data — Special Handling

Data you submit about your clients is treated with the highest level of confidentiality. We do not:

  • Share client data with third parties for any commercial purpose
  • Use client data to train our AI models without explicit consent
  • Store client data beyond the period required to generate your outputs

You are responsible for ensuring you have the necessary consent and authority to submit client data to our platform under applicable financial regulations in your jurisdiction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with:

  • Paddle: Our payment processor, for billing purposes only
  • Infrastructure providers: Hosting and cloud services operating under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal authorities: Where required by applicable law

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and secure data storage. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription and up to 90 days after cancellation. Submitted client data used for output generation is deleted within 30 days of output delivery. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights: velago.app@gmail.com

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the platform to function — specifically session authentication and preference storage. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. International Users

VelaGo operates globally. By using the Service, you consent to the processing of your information in the country where our infrastructure is hosted. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for cross-border data transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests: velago.app@gmail.com
