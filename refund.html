Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  NEA  ·  velago.app

This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which NEA ("Company") provides refunds for VelaGo subscriptions. We are committed to being fair, transparent, and reasonable.

We offer a 7-day refund window on your first payment. If VelaGo is not right for you, contact us within 7 days of your first charge and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

1. First-Payment Guarantee

If you are a new subscriber and are unsatisfied with the Service for any reason, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your first payment. This applies to all subscription tiers: Core ($99), Authority ($299), Elite ($799), and Concierge ($3,000).

To request a refund under this guarantee, email velago.app@gmail.com within 7 days of your charge with the subject line "Refund Request" and your account email.

2. Recurring Monthly Payments

After the first-payment guarantee period, monthly subscription payments are non-refundable. This is because:

  • Outputs are generated and delivered at the time of submission
  • Our costs are incurred at the point of delivery
  • You retain full ownership of all outputs delivered

You may cancel your subscription at any time to stop future charges. Cancellation does not trigger a refund for the current billing period.

3. Service Failure Refunds

If we fail to deliver your requested outputs within the stated 24-hour window, or if outputs are materially defective and cannot be corrected upon revision, you are entitled to:

  • A credit toward your next billing cycle, or
  • A pro-rated refund for the affected period, at our discretion

To report a service failure, contact us at velago.app@gmail.com within 7 days of the missed or defective delivery.

4. Concierge Plan ($3,000/month)

Concierge plan refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis given the custom nature of the deliverables. We commit to resolving all Concierge refund requests within 5 business days of receipt.

5. How Refunds Are Processed

Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method via Paddle within 5–10 business days, depending on your card issuer or payment provider.

6. Contact for Refund Requests

All refund requests must be submitted by email to: velago.app@gmail.com

Include in your email:

  • Your account email address
  • The subscription tier you subscribed to
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Your Paddle transaction ID (found in your payment confirmation email)

We respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.
